Processor flexibility

Cloud9 describes its gateway as processor agnostic and publishes support for major processors including Chase Paymentech, Elavon, Global Payments, Heartland Payment Systems, NUVEI, TSYS, TransFirst, Vantiv, USAT, and WorldPay in the USA, plus NUVEI, TSYS, and Global in Canada.

Support options and processor availability can vary by setup and region.

Included business tools

Member Portal & virtual payments: securely collect payments through a hosted portal or one-time payment page.

Member Portal Video

The Cloud9 Gateway includes a Member Portal that can be added to an email or website by linking it to a merchant account configured on the gateway. This gives merchants a secure way to collect and manage payments online.

A one-time virtual payment page can also be sent by email for remote payment collection. When a payment is captured, the merchant can receive an email notification for immediate visibility.
